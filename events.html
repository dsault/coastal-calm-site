Venue & Catering Options

We offer flexible packages so you can choose what works best for your event:

  • Venue only – Use our spaces and bring your own caterer / setup
  • Venue + Catering – Full service from our restaurant kitchen (buffet or plated)
  • Partial catering – We handle main courses; you arrange drinks or desserts
  • Full event package – Venue, catering, basic setup, and coordination support

Our restaurant can prepare Filipino favorites, international dishes, and special menus. Scenic dining is available throughout the resort.
