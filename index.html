Restaurant & Dining

Good Food. Great View. Best Moments

Open daily 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Enjoy Filipino favorites, pizza, pasta, sandwiches, and more while taking in ocean views — plus great steaks you thought you could only get in Cebu. From sisig and sinigang to ribeye and specialty pizzas — something for everyone.

Restaurant food is available throughout the resort, so you can choose from many ideal scenic dining locations — by the pool, under the gazebo, or with a direct view of the sea.
