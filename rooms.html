Our Room Types

We offer seven comfortable room types. All rooms feature queen beds.

Standard Room

Comfortable and well-appointed room with modern amenities and easy access to the pool and main facilities.

Best for: Solo travelers & short stays

Deluxe Room

Spacious room with upgraded furnishings and extra comfort for a relaxing stay.

Best for: Couples & leisure travelers

Deluxe with Jet Bath

Deluxe room featuring a relaxing jet bath tub — perfect for unwinding after a day by the sea.

Best for: Couples wanting extra indulgence

Deluxe Ocean View

Enjoy beautiful sea views from your room with upgraded amenities and a queen bed.

Best for: Those who love waking up to the ocean

Deluxe Deck

Deluxe room with private deck area for outdoor relaxation and fresh sea breeze.

Best for: Guests who enjoy private outdoor space

Family Room

Spacious room with one queen bed and bunk beds — ideal for families with children.

Best for: Families & groups with kids

Adjoining Rooms

Two connected rooms (each with queen beds) that offer flexibility for families or larger groups while keeping everyone close.

Best for: Families or groups who want connected space
